Get the best out of the giving your tithes.

Before you give your tithes at church, a daily devotional time with God to have fulfilments in the 5 Christian Persuasions of Church Tithing will add to growth in your trusting and loving relations with Him

As a Church tither, Tithing to Grow in Chris is a quality devotional time you'll have with God, before giving your tithes at church.

The Devotional App is designed to assist you in spiritually relating to God personally in the giving of your tithes. As you use this devotional guide, before presenting your tithes to God at church, you wil soon find yourself experiencing seven things. These seven things are as follows:
  • Getting closer to God
  • Thinking about God
  • Praying to God
  • Reading about God
  • Honoring God
  • Giving to God
  • Hearing from God

The whole experience is designed to provide the grounds to grow in your trusting and loving relationship with God, and being holy to Him.

Making it Count for Your Christian Growth

Traditionally, what we know and are accustomed to in the giving of our tithes is to put the tithes in a designated envelop, and then drop it in whatever is used for the collection. We usually go about this without a prior devotional time with God.
Tithing to Grow in Christ is a new approach to the giving of our tithes, which we are to accommodate with the goal to grow in our trusting and loving relations with God.
Tithing to Grow in Christ comes out of Abraham’s tithing, which was followed by growth in his trusting and loving relations with God, and in being holier to Him.
Tithing to Grow in Christ makes it necessary to have a quality devotional time with God regarding the Five Persuasions of Church Tithing, before we give the tithes at church.
The diligence we give to the ritual of having a prior quality devotional time with God regarding the Five Persuasions of Church Tithing is how we make the giving of our tithes count for growth in our trusting and loving relations with God.
